Dodge

This game has been tested for working in Chrome and Safari. Performance in other browsers is not guaranteed.

Playing is easy. Just try and survive longer than the other player by not hitting the walls or the obstacles or coming at you. Watch out for when the game starts changing, though.

How to play:
Player 1 uses the S key to control their character and dodge obstacles. Player 2 uses the L key to control their character and dodge obstacles. Hold your key to propel yourself away from the pull of gravity.
